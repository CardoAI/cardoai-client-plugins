# Commercial — Revenue Based Finance — Enum / Allowed Values

### investments — Sales Reporting Frequency

How often the borrower is required to report revenue (e.g. monthly, quarterly).

Allowed values:
- Monthly | Quarterly | Other

### investments — Repayment Cap Reached Flag

Indicates whether the repayment cap has been reached on this loan.

Allowed values:
- True | False

### investments — Loan Purpose

Purpose of the loan as per defined list of values.

Allowed values:
- Appliance or Furniture | Debt Consolidation | Equipment | Home Improvement | Living Expenses | Medical | Merger and Acquisition | New Car | New Information Technology Investment | New Plant and Equipment Investment | Other | Other Expansionary Purpose | Other Vehicle | Overdraft or Working Capital | Property | Refurbishment of Existing Plant Equipment or Technology | Travel | Tuition | Used Car | Working Capital

### investments — Amortisation Method

Method used to repay the loan (e.g. annuity, bullet, linear).

Allowed values:
- Annuity | Bullet | Linear | Revenue Share | Interest Only | Other

### investments — Payment Frequency

Scheduled frequency of principal payments.

Allowed values:
- Monthly | Quarterly | Semi-Annually | Annually | Daily | Weekly | Other

### investments — Is Closed

Flag indicating whether the loan account has been fully closed / repaid.

Allowed values:
- True | False

### investments — Performance Status

Performance status of the loan based on transaction-specific contractual definition.

Allowed values:
- Performing | In Delay | Default | Grace Period | Full Recovery | In Stock | Other

### investments — Performance Status Contractual

Performance status based strictly on contractual payment obligations, without applying any grace periods.

Allowed values:
- Performing | In Delay | Default | Grace Period | Full Recovery | In Stock | Other

### investments — RBF Status

Terminal repayment / resolution status of the loan.

Allowed values:
- Fully Repaid | No Recovery | No Repayment | Partially Repaid | Repurchased | Write-off No Recovery | Write-off Partial Recovery

### investments — Default Flag

Indicator showing whether the loan is in default.

Allowed values:
- True | False

### asset_modification — Loan Modified Flag

Indicates whether the loan has been modified.

Allowed values:
- True | False

### asset_modification — Modified Type

Type of modification applied to the loan.

Allowed values:
- Collateral Modification | Extension | Interest Rate Change | No Modification | Ope Legis Suspension | Partial Early Repayment | Payment Suspension | Principal Forgiveness | Principal Suspension | Renegotiation | Renegotiation With Extension | Rescheduling | Suspension With Extension | Suspension Without Extension

### borrower — Enterprise Size

Size category of the enterprise (e.g. micro, SME, large).

Allowed values:
- Micro | Small | Medium | Large

### borrower — Is Sole Proprietorship

Indicates if the company is a sole proprietorship.

Allowed values:
- True | False

### cash_flows — Type

type of cash flow as per defined list of values

Allowed values:
- General Repayment | Principal Repayment | Interest | Fee | Prepayment | Recovery
